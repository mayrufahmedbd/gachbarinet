Our Story

From humble beginnings to village connectivity leaders

2021

The Idea Was Born

Founded by local entrepreneurs who saw the need for reliable internet in rural West Bengal after struggling with poor connectivity during the pandemic.

2022

First Tower Installed

Set up our first wireless tower in Gachbari village, providing internet to 50 homes and local businesses.

2023

Expanded Coverage

Grew our network to serve 10 villages with over 500 happy customers and counting.

2024

Future Goals

Planning to expand to 50+ villages and introduce fiber optic connections for even faster speeds.
