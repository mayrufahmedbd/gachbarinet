Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a new connection?

You can apply for a new connection by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our sales team, or visiting our office. We'll guide you through the simple process which includes verifying service availability in your area and scheduling installation.

What documents are required for connection?

You'll need to provide:

  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/Passport)
  • Proof of address (Recent utility bill/Rental agreement)
  • One passport-size photograph

How long does installation take?

For areas within our current coverage, installation is typically completed within 2-3 working days after payment confirmation. In new areas where infrastructure needs to be extended, it may take 1-2 weeks depending on the location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods:

  • Cash (at our office or through field agents)
  • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, PayTM)
  • Bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS)
  • Credit/Debit cards (at office only)

How can I check if my area is covered?

You can check our coverage map on the Coverage page or call our support team with your exact location details. We're constantly expanding our network to serve more villages.
